R&D Spending and Pharmaceutical Innovation: A Cross-Country Panel Analysis

May 2026
  • Built a 220-observation country-year panel (20 countries, 2010–2020) merging World Bank WDI macroeconomic indicators with ClinicalTrials.gov trial counts in R, handling missing R&D data via linear interpolation and constant-fill methods.
  • Estimated pooled OLS models of clinical trial activity on R&D spending, GDP per capita, population, and health expenditure, with two robustness checks (excluding imputed countries; zero-filling missing values) to test sensitivity of the R&D effect to data handling decisions.

Field Experiment

Worker Incentive Field Experiment

Dec 2025
  • Designed and ran a natural field experiment on Prolific (n=40) using Qualtrics to test piece-rate vs. flat-rate incentive contracts on worker output quantity and quality under controlled conditions.
  • Cleaned and analyzed response data in Excel using t-tests and OLS regression controlling for demographic variables — piece-rate contracts raised output by ~1.4 words (p=0.029) with no statistically significant quality trade-off, consistent with standard piece-rate theory.
